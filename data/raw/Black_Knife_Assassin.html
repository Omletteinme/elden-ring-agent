Background[]

Scions of the Eternal Cities, the Black Knife Assassins were a group of women with close ties to Queen Marika the Eternal.[1] Led by Alecto, Black Knife Ringleader, they were coconspirators of Ranni the Witch, who robbed the Rune of Death from Maliketh, the Black Blade.[2] She imbued it into Blades of Calling, fashioning a set of god-slaying Black Knives.[3][4] The Assassins carried out the Night of Black Knives, carving Cursemarks of Death into Ranni and Godwyn the Golden.[5] They wore silent, silver scale armor and used Concealing Veils to mask their presence, rendering them effectively invisible.[6][7][8]

Presently, they are scattered throughout the Lands Between, and can be found in many different regions.

Background[]

This assassin sustained a grievous wound, retreating deep within the Deathtouched Catacombs. Uniquely, her health bar has already been partially depleted before the player arrives so that she starts at 60% HP. She is the only wounded Black Knife Assassin. Although red blood stains her armor, she bleeds darkness on hit.

She drops the Assassin's Crimson Dagger, a Talisman which allows the player to recover HP after performing a critical hit.

Background[]

Main article: Alecto, Black Knife Ringleader
The ringleader of the Black Knife Assassins is currently imprisoned in an Evergaol in the Moonlight Altar area of Liurnia of the Lakes. Tiche, Alecto's daughter, died protecting her mother during their flight from the Capital after the assassination of Godwyn.[9]

Notes[]

  • Black Knife Assassin enemies are unmarked bosses that do not respawn once defeated.
  • Their bodies are invisible.
  • They float when they backstep, as if manipulated by unseen strings.
  • Melina uses the Black Knife Assassin model and animations, noticeable when summoned as a cooperator against Morgott, the Omen King. She wields the Blade of Calling, and may cast Minor Erdtree and Miranda's Prayer.
  • Black Knife Assassins have immunity to all Status Effects except Frostbite, corroborating Destined Death's vulnerability to the Eclipse.[10]
