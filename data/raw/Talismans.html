Talismans are special items in Elden Ring. They can be equipped to provide a variety of effects.

Note that talismans with variants (like +1 or +2 after the name) will only allow one of these to be equipped at a time, so one can't equip both Arsenal Charm and Arsenal Charm +1, even if one has enough talisman slots for both.

You begin the game with the ability to equip 1 talisman at a time. This number can be increased by acquiring Talisman Pouches.
