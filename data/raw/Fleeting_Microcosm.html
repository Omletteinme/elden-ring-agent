A finger sorcery of Count Ymir, High Priest. One of the secret hallowed rites.

Conjures an image of a microcosm at a foe's feet that pulses with a single wave before disappearing in a burst. Charging enhances potency.

The broken and discarded are fully willing to cling to fleeting simulacra, earning them some modicum of sympathy.

Acquisition[]

Purchased: Cathedral of Manus Metyr

  • Fleeting Microcosm may be purchased from Count Ymir for 20,000 Runes after ringing the bell at the Finger Ruins of Dheo. He sits on the throne in the Cathedral of Manus Metyr on the Scadu Altus, located in the Realm of Shadow.

Effects[]

The caster summons a microcosm at the targetвАЩs feet, dealing damage on pulse and again when it bursts. It inflicts Magic damage with an Attack Power of 120/214% of Incantation Scaling and 7/7 Stance damage within a 3.0/4.5-meter radius.

Charging the spell increases its potency to 160/271% Incantation Scaling and 7/11 Stance damage within a 3.0/6.0-meter radius.

Notes[]

  • Costs 26 FP per cast.
  • Costs 35 Stamina per cast (46 when charged).
  • Requires 1 Memory Slot to be equipped.
  • This spell deals damage twice.
  • The burst may knock down enemies.
  • This spell cannot be cast while riding Torrent.

Boosting[]

  • Finger Robe boosts attack power by 8%.
  • Maternal Staff boosts attack power by 15%.
  • Godfrey Icon boosts the attack power of charged attacks by 15%.

Trivia[]

  • Fleeting Microcosm is a recreation of the microcosm held between the tail fingers of Metyr and the Staff of the Great Beyond. Metyr's microcosm once relayed information from the Greater Will.[1]
