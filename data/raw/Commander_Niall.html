Commander Niall is an optional boss in Elden Ring. Defeating him will unlock the Commander Niall achievement.

Overview[]

Commander Niall, a veteran general of Castle Sol, offered his prosthesis in exchange for the lives of imprisoned knights, forming an army of no nation.[1] A lone survivor, Niall commands the spirits of deceased knights to defend his master, who has long passed.[2] Niall guards a chest containing the Haligtree Secret Medallion (Left), one-half of the Medallion required to gain access to the Consecrated Snowfield, the land of Miquella's Haligtree.

Moveset[]

At the beginning of the fight, Niall summons the spirits of two Banished Knights, one with a sword and shield, and the other wielding dual swords. Niall will be relatively passive while the knights are alive, instead buffing them until they have both been defeated or his health has been reduced to half. Both Banished Knights will inflict Frostbite with their attacks. Niall himself uses a mixture of lightning and frost-based attacks. He can imbue his prosthesis with lightning and perform kicks and stomps, and he can use his halberd to generate frost.
