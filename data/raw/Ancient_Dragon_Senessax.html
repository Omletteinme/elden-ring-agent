Ancient Dragon Senessax is an optional boss in Shadow of the Erdtree encountered inside the Jagged Peak.

Background[]

An ancient dragon that can be found sleeping at Jagged Peak, Senessax is implied to have sided with Bayle the Dread, as some of the ancient dragons who betrayed their lord Placidusax to side with Bayle were known to wield branching red lightning.[1]

Appearance[]

Like other ancient dragons, it has four limbs and four wings.

Gallery[]

Senessax is found sleeping.
Like other ancient dragons, it can wield lightning.
