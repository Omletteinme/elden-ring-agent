Carian Phalanx is a spell in Elden Ring. A vastly improved version of Glintblade Phalanx with double the blades, that automatically fire at hostile enemies that get too close and they inflict good Stance damage. Cannot be used at the same time as Glintblade Phalanx, casting either spell replaces the effect of the previous one.

It is one of the Carian Blade Sorceries, a subset of the Carian Sorceries.
