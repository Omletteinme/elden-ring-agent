Overview[]

A traitorous Bloodhound Knight hiding within an evergaol cell, and is sought out by Blaidd the Half-Wolf.[1] Summoning Blaidd the Half-Wolf (only possible after having used the Finger Snap gesture to speak with him at Mistwood Ruins) and letting him take on the brunt of the boss's attacks, while occasionally sneaking in a jumping attack, is a very efficient way to kill this knight.

Notes[]

  • While Darriwil has a very low amount of HP, even for being found this early in the game, his aggressive nature and weirdly delayed jump attacks and dragging charges can be rather difficult to read.

Trivia[]

  • This boss replaced Crucible Knight Floh, who was the boss of this location early in development.
