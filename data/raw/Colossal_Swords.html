Colossal Swords are a type of melee armament in Elden Ring.

Overview[]

They have excellent reach and area coverage, along with high damage against both health and poise, at the cost of their high weight and slow attack speed. Its one-handed moveset's slow speed is at risk of getting parried; however, most moves of its two-handed moveset are completely un-parriable.

Most Colossal Swords require a decent amount of investment into the Strength attribute in order to be wielded; however, it's possible to still wield a colossal sword with less strength than listed through two-handing, as two-handing increases the player's effective Strength by 50%.
