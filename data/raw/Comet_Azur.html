Legendary sorcery devised by Azur, primeval sorcerer.

Fires a tremendous comet in a torrent akin to the distant starry expanse, the place said to be the origin of glintstone. Hold to continue releasing the sorcery's power.

When Azur glimpsed into the primeval current, he saw darkness. He was left both bewitched and fearful of the abyss.

Acquisition[]

Quest Item: Hermit Village

  • Comet Azur is obtained by interacting with Primeval Sorcerer Azur, found on the cliffs southeast of the Hermit Village in Mt. Gelmir.

Effects[]

The caster channels for 3 seconds before releasing a torrent of energy that deals Magic damage, with an Attack Power equal to ???% of Sorcery Scaling per second.[citation needed] The beam continues as long as the casting button is held, consuming additional FP.

Notes[]

  • Costs 46 FP per cast and drains 10 FP per sustained increment.[citation needed]
  • Costs 34 Stamina per cast.
  • Requires 3 Memory Slots to equip.
  • Can be channeled until FP is depleted.
  • Deals low Stance damage, but the beam's end deals more.

Boosting[]

  • Azur's Glintstone Crown boosts attack power and FP cost by 15%.
  • Azur's Glintstone Staff reduces casting time and increases FP costs by 20%.
  • Cerulean Hidden Tear removes FP costs for 15 seconds.
  • Glintstone Scarab reduces FP cost by 15%.
  • The Graven-School Talisman and Graven-Mass Talisman boost attack power by 4% and 8% respectively, stacking for a total of 12%.
  • Magic Scorpion Charm boosts attack power by 12% (8% in PvP).
  • Magic-Shrouding Cracked Tear boosts attack power by 20% (15% in PvP).
  • Primal Glintstone Blade reduces FP cost by 25%.
  • Radagon Icon reduces casting time by ??%.
  • Terra Magica boosts attack power by 35%.

Strategies[]

  • The beam has long range and can pass through enemies.
  • The beam’s direction cannot be adjusted once cast.
  • Casting time is reduced by Dexterity, up to a maximum of 70 Dexterity.
  • If cast immediately after applying the Cerulean Hidden Tear, it can be channeled without FP drain for up to 12 seconds.
  • This spell is easily interrupted due to its long casting time.
  • Most bosses and enemies will dodge when the spell is initially cast, not when the beam is released.

Trivia[]

  • Despite the academy's ban on primeval sorceries,[1] Comet Azur is used by Rennala, Queen of the Full Moon.
