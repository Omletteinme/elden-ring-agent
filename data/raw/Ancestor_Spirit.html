The Ancestor Spirit (祖霊 , Sorei, Ancestral Spirit) is an optional boss in Elden Ring. It also appears as a field boss in Elden Ring Nightreign.

Background[]

The antler-like horns of a long-lived stag continued budding long after death, over and over again, until the beast became an Ancestor Spirit. Ancestral Spirits are a phenomenon that exists beyond the purview of the Erdtree, following the principle that life sprouts from death, just as it does birth, granting power. Its horned remains are found at the southern Hallowhorn Grounds on the bank of the Siofra River, where it lies beneath the false night sky. Its large, antler-like horns sprouted small leafy plants that shone with light, infusing nearby Ancestral Followers with profound spirituality.[1][2][3][4]

Overview[]

  • This boss is optional.
  • Furled Fingers or Spirit Ashes can be summoned for this boss.
  • Deals Standard and Magic damage

Drops[]

  • 13,000 runes
  • Ancestral Follower Ashes

Location[]

The horned remains of this Ancestor Spirit are found at the Hallowhorn Grounds in the lower section of the Siofra River, accessible via the Siofra River Well in Limgrave. This ruined structure is situated southeast of the Siofra River Bank Site of Grace.

In order to face this boss, the player must first light eight beacons scattered throughout the area. Each beacon corresponds to a pillar flanking the staircase to the Hallowhorn Grounds. Once all signals are lit, the following message displays: Power gathers somewhere in horned remains. The horned remains will burned with blue spectral flames, and agreeing to Touch horned remains will grant a supernatural encounter with the spirit.

Stats[]

  • HP: 4,393
  • Defense: 107
  • Stance: 120
  • This boss is vulnerable to a Critical Attack after its stance is broken.
  • Cannot be parried
  • Ancestor Spirit is scripted to absorb the souls of nearby animal spirits, gaining a new attack based on the aspect. It periodically die, rebirthing itself, losing the move.

Moveset[]

This page has missing data (moveset). Please add it.

The Ancestor Spirit is able to jump up and hop while mid-air. It can produce a jet of blue spirit vapor from its mouth, both while on the ground and in the air.

Notes[]

  • Defeating this boss for the first time unlocks the "Ancestor Spirit" achievement.
  • This is the weaker version of the Regal Ancestor Spirit.
  • Due to their similarities in design and behavior, Torrent may be a juvenile Ancestor Spirit.

Trivia[]

  • In Norse mythology, four four stags eat among the branches of the world tree Yggdrasill. According to the Poetic Edda, the stags crane their necks upward to chomp at the branches. The morning dew gathers in their horns and forms the rivers of the world.
