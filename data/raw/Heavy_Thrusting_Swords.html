Heavy Thrusting Swords are a type of melee armament in Elden Ring.

Overview[]

They have moderately quick attack speed and reasonable range as well as more damage against health, poise and have a relatively subtle telegraphs at the start of most of their attack animations. However they are overall slower and have a higher stamina cost compared to smaller Thrusting Swords

Similar to Curved Swords and Thrusting Swords, their heavy attack charges can be canceled in favor of a quick feint-attack should the player press the Roll button while charging. They can also attack while guarding at the same time similar to smaller to their smaller brethren and Spears. Their two-handed moveset is generally very similar to their one-handed one, increasing overall damage to health and poise.
