Background[]

A hulking troll knight clad in heavy armor and wielding a massive greatsword, Bols was one of the Trolls called into service by the Carians, fighting as a true enchanted knight alongside human comrades.[1] Bols was imprisoned within the Cuckoo's Evergaol in western Liurnia of the Lakes by the Knights of the Cuckoo during the Liurnian Civil War.

Characteristics[]

A towering troll knight with slow, sweeping greatsword attacks that create shockwaves. His size makes it easy to pass between his legs and circle to his back. He can summon Greatblade Phalanx blades overhead.

Moveset[]

Bols, Carian Knight deals Standard damage, Slash damage, Pierce damage, and Magic damage.

Moveset
Attack Description
Stomp Raises one leg and stomps repeatedly. Roll toward the leg that did not stomp and punish.
Swing Horizontal or vertical greatsword swings. Roll into horizontal; roll away from vertical.
Arm Sweep Low arm sweep along the ground. Roll into the arm.
Arm Slam One or both arms slam, producing a shockwave. Get behind him or jump the wave.
Downward Stab Drives the blade into the ground, large shockwave. Get behind him during the wind-up.
Sword Wrench Rips the sword free to his right after Downward Stab. Stay on his left.
Kneeling Swing Kneels and performs a wide counter-clockwise sweep. Roll toward the weapon.
Mega Slam Long hold overhead slam with huge shockwave. Safe spot is directly behind; or jump the wave.
Jumping Slam Leaps and lands with a shockwave. Roll on descent.
Dragging Slash Long wind-up dash dragging the blade, then slash and follow-ups ending in a slam. Slip between his legs or get behind him; luring him into the arena edge can stop and stun the charge.
Greatblade Phalanx Summons three blades above his head that fire after a delay. Run toward him so the blades overshoot; avoid pairing attempts since only one blade can be parried at a time.
Roar Shockwave roar that buffs his damage by 20% for ~15s. Get behind him if close; otherwise create distance.

Strategies[]

  • Use his size: dodge between his legs and stay on his back side.
  • Keep him in view; he can pivot quickly into a follow-up.
  • Stay close even for ranged builds; create distance only to reset spacing.
  • When he casts Greatblade Phalanx, sprint toward him so the blades pass overhead.
  • Shockwaves from slams fan out in front; positioning behind him mitigates most follow-ups.
