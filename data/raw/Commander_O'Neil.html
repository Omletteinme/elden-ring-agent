Overview[]

A veteran of a battle that took place in the Swamp of Aeonia in Caelid. He continued to brandish his flag in the rot-eaten battlefield even after his lord fled.[1] He wears the same armor and wields the same standard as Commander Niall, the leader of an army of no nation situated in Castle Sol.

In battle, Commander O'Neil summons the spirits of Exile Soldiers. Initially, he will summon five regular soldiers, and will summon three large soldiers after his health has been reduced to half. His attacks inflict Scarlet Rot.

O'Neil is in possession of the Unalloyed Gold Needle, which is required by Sage Gowry to stanch Millicent's Rot.

Polyanna, Adopted Daughter can be summoned for this fight.
