Overview[]

Scholars of the Haima Conspectus of the Academy of Raya Lucaria. Haima scholars are the adjudicators of the Academy,[1] who studied sorceries of cannon and gavel in order to gain the power to quell conflict.[2] Whenever war broke out and the Academy sealed its gates, the battlemages of Haima were the only scholars who dared venture outside.[3] As such, they can be found in many regions of the Lands Between. The stone clubs they carry reflect their more forceful approach, allowing them to feel the impact of their blows so they do not lose sight of the true essence of battle.[4]
