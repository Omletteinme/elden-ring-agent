Overview[]

Cemetery Shades are shadowy, insect-ridden gravekeepers found in catacombs. They are lanky, long-necked humanoids with hollow torsos and large, glowing eyes. Each has a creature resembling a spider or a crab sitting atop their head, and they fight with pairs of curved blades.[1]
