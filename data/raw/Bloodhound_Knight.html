Overview[]

Bloodhound Knights are trained as hunters and are known to be unshakable trackers. Without language, they choose their own master whom they will stay loyal to for the rest of their life.[1]

They wield a Bloodhound's Fang alongside a Bloodhound Claw that allows them to slip past an enemy's guard. They use the Ash of War: Bloodhound's Step to either move out of harm's way or to close the gap between them and their opponent quickly.

Lesser variants can be found as non-respawning enemies guarding corpses in the overworld. One is found atop a wall near the gate past the Isolated Merchant at Academy of Raya Lucaria, another is in one of the rooms past the illusory wall in Volcano Manor, a third is guarding Eternal Darkness in Swamp Lookout Tower atop the gate wall near Sellia, Town of Sorcery, and a fourth is guarding the Gelmir Knight Set inside of Gelmir Hero's Grave in a room reached by dropping onto rafters below the Chariot's path.

Background[]

A Bloodhound Knight was found at the Lakeside Crystal Cave exit. It appears to be guarding the location of Latenna the Albinauric, forcing her to stay until the location of the Haligtree Secret Medallion (Right) has been revealed.

Notes[]

  • Wears Bloodhound Knight Helm, Bloodhound Knight Armor, Bloodhound Knight Gauntlets, and Bloodhound Knight Greaves
  • Wields Bloodhound's Fang and Bloodhound Claws
  • Uses Ash of War: Bloodhound's Step
  • The emblem on their cape is a wolf's head surrounded by concentric thorned rings.
