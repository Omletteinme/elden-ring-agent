Background[]

Lansseax, sister of Fortissax, was an Ancient Dragon that took the form of a human priestess to commune with the knights of Leyndell's Ancient Dragon Cult.[1] Her most beloved knight was Vyke the Dragonspear,[2] a Tarnished knight of the Roundtable Hold, who was later scorched by the Frenzied Flame.[3]

Location[]

Lansseax is first encountered near the Abandoned Coffin Site of Grace, ambushing the player near the entrance to Altus Plateau from the Ruin-Strewn Precipice. After sustaining enough damage, she abruptly ends the fight by flying away.

Her next encounter is on a hill southwest of the Rampartside Path Site of Grace, located in eastern Altus Plateau, where she can be defeated for good.

Drops[]

Lansseax's Glaive 100%

Moveset[]

Lansseax has two unique moves that involve her summoning a glaive made of red lightning. One is a diagonal slam which releases waves of lightning across the ground that can be dodged through. The other is a horizontal sweep which also sends out waves.
