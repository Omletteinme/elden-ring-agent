Black Knight Edredd is an optional boss in Shadow of the Erdtree. They are encountered at Fort of Reprimand, accessed via the Scadu Altus.

Background[]

Edredd was a member of the Black Knights who crusaded with Messmer the Impaler in the Shadow Realm.[1] The Black Knights were notorious for their unwavering conviction to endless slaughter in a war without honor.[2][3] Edredd never received the Grace of Gold.[4]

Appearance[]

A sturdy knight with a Black Steel Twinblade wearing black iron armor accented with gold detailing.[1] The armor bears the crest of the Scadutree engraved in gold.[4]
