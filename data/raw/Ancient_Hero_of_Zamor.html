Overview[]

Main article: Zamor Knight

The three ancient heroes of Zamor earned renown during the War Against the Giants, where they finally triumphed over their mortal enemies.[1] They hail from the Town of Zamor, and cloak themselves in icy tempests.[2] They appear as tall, slender humanoids with long, silver hair and ornate masks.

Background[]

A Zamor knight was graced by Lord Radagon.[3] They were sealed within an evergaol between the Fourth Church of Marika and the Church of Pilgrimage for unknown reasons.

It carries Radagon's Scarseal and is found in the Weeping Evergaol, which must be unlocked with a Stonesword Key.
